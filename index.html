OVERVIEW

A unified model for all audio

Unlike other models that are "blind" to context, Bagpiper builds a deep semantic understanding of every sound it hears. It uses this cognitive reasoning to solve open-ended tasks, whether you need to analyze a complex acoustic scene or synthesize a multi-speaker dialogue with music and effects.
